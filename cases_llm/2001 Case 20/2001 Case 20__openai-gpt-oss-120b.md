### 4.  Pathophysiologic Mechanisms Linking the Findings

#### 4.2.  Endothelial Injury and Bacterial Adhesion

Certain Gram‑negative organisms express adhesins (e.g., OmpA, pili) that bind to extracellular matrix proteins.  Although specific adhesins for *Moraxella* have not been delineated, experimental data suggest that *Moraxella* can adhere to damaged epithelium and to prosthetic material.  This provides a plausible route for **bacterial colonization of the plaque**, subsequent replication, and **continuous seeding of the bloodstream**.
